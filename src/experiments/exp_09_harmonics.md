## Physics Background

The lattice produces discrete mass spectrum through resonance effects:

- **Arnold Tongues**: Frequency locking between particle and vacuum
- **Dispersion Relations**: ω(k) for different lattice directions
- **Brillouin Zone**: Band structure from lattice periodicity
- **Quantization**: Discrete frequencies from resonance locking

- **Frequency Sweeps**: Scan ω vs. k for different directions
- **Resonance Detection**: Identify Arnold tongue boundaries
- **Dispersion Measurement**: ω(k) relations for photons and massive particles

### Measurements
- **Mass Spectrum**: Discrete frequencies from resonance locking

## Results

### Quantization Effects
- **Discrete Masses**: Frequency locking produces quantized spectrum
- **Arnold Tongues**: Finite-width stability regions
- **No Boundary Conditions**: Quantization from resonance dynamics

### Dispersion Relations
