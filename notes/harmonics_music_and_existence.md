The non-smooth boundary of the bright resonance corridor — the Arnold tongue

all close up consistently in a finite cycle. This is why you cannot tune a piano
to pure fifths and have the octaves still work. The incommensurability is real
and unavoidable.

the "wolf interval" that lives between irrationals, where no pure resonance exists.

The dark gaps in the harmonic landscape are the wolf intervals of spacetime.

the wolf interval exists in tuning theory: the irrationals between rational
resonances are structurally excluded from stable locking.
