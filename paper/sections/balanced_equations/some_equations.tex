\section*{1. Unified Entanglement-Balance Equation}

We define the entanglement variable $\mathcal{E}_{i,j}$ between lattice sites $i$ and $j$.
Its time evolution is governed by creation, loss, and probability-mediated transfer:



\[
\dot{\mathcal{E}}_{i,j}
=
\underbrace{\mathcal{C}_{i,j}}_{\text{creation}}
-
\underbrace{\mathcal{D}_{i,j}}_{\text{loss}}
+
\underbrace{
\sum_{k}
\left(
\Pi_{k\to (i,j)} - \Pi_{(i,j)\to k}
\right)
}_{\text{entanglement transfer}}
\]



Expanded in terms of explicit rates:



\[
\dot{\mathcal{E}}_{i,j}
=
\dot{\mathcal{E}}_{i,j}^{(+)}
-
\dot{\mathcal{E}}_{i,j}^{(-)}
+
\sum_{k}
\left(
\dot{\Pi}_{k\to (i,j)} - \dot{\Pi}_{(i,j)\to k}
\right)
\]



Including decoherence or emission:



\[
\dot{\mathcal{E}}_{i,j}
=
\mathcal{C}_{i,j}
-
\gamma_{i,j}\,\mathcal{E}_{i,j}
+
\sum_{k}
\left(
\Pi_{k\to (i,j)} - \Pi_{(i,j)\to k}
\right)
\]

\section*{2. Probability-Flux Continuity Equation}

Let $\rho_i$ denote the probability density at lattice site $i$.
Local conservation of probability yields the discrete continuity equation:



\[
\dot{\rho}_i
=
\sum_{j \in \mathcal{N}(i)}
\left(
\Pi_{j\to i} - \Pi_{i\to j}
\right)
\]



In flux form:



\[
\dot{\rho}_i
=
-\sum_{j \in \mathcal{N}(i)} \mathbf{J}_{i\to j}
\]



Where the probability current may be driven by force-channel gradients:



\[
\mathbf{J}_{i\to j}
=
\sum_{f \in \{G,C,W,S\}}
\alpha_{(f)}\,
\nabla_{(f)} \rho \cdot \hat{e}_{i\to j}
\]
