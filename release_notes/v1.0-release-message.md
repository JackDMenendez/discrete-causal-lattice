# v1.0 — Closes the RC cycle: Born rule via Gleason, explicit g_μν metric, refined SM-gauge automorphism conjecture

The first non-beta release of *Geometry First*.  v1.0 closes the
release-candidate cycle that started with v0.95-RC.  Three new
Tier-1/Tier-2 proof items are in the paper at the
sufficiency-with-pointers level, three new audit-table rows reflect
new experimental work and corrected accounting of the
SM-derivation claim, six computational scaffolding scripts establish
each component of the central conjecture, and the title-page
metadata + page layout went through a paragraph-by-paragraph audit
pass.

This is **Paper I of the A=1 Discrete Causal Lattice series**.

## Why this is v1.0 and not another RC

v0.98-RC opened a new question (long-horizon two-body stability)
that the paper did not yet answer.  v1.0 answers it: `exp_12c`
identifies the $65^3$ "stability" as a finite-volume artefact
(boundary reflections confining the wave packet near $R_1$); true
orbital lifetime on grids $\geq 81^3$ is around tick 140.  The
follow-up `exp_12d_tight` cross-validates `exp_12`'s 4-sig-fig
PASS across grids $\{65, 81, 97, 113\}^3$ and finds the lock-in
attractor is grid-independent.

The framework's verified scope at v1.0 is now sharply defined:
$\mathrm{Aut}(\Tdiamond, \mathcal{A}=1) \supseteq SO(3,1) \times
U(1)$ (dim 7) on the existing per-site $\mathbb{C}^2$ amplitude,
proven by computational verification.  The full Standard Model
gauge-group conjecture is precisely scoped as a Lie-algebra
equality requiring per-site $\mathbb{C}^2$ isospin and
$\mathbb{C}^3$ colour structural extensions — the central open
problem and the explicit subject of the planned companion paper
(follow-on #16).

## Headline changes

### Three new audit-table rows

- **Long-horizon escape mechanism** (`exp_12c`, `PART`) — orbital
  escape at tick ~140 on $\geq 81^3$, ~2000 on $65^3$; the $65^3$
  result was a finite-volume artefact.  Init-mode and drift-mode
  controls rule out alternative causes.
- **Lock-in resonance grid-independence** (`exp_12d_tight` +
  `exp_12d_outlier_trace`, `PART`) — $r$ at $k_\text{Bohr} =
  0.0971$ in $[11.54, 12.30]$ across all four grids
  ($\Delta r \approx 0.8$); three of four $k_\text{min}$ within
  $\pm 0.003$ of $k_\text{Bohr}$.  Cross-grid validation of
  `exp_12`'s 4-sig-fig PASS.  Two outliers traced to early-escape
  tail of `exp_12c`'s metastable-orbit mechanism, not a
  $k=0.101$-specific instability.
- **Standard Model gauge group as automorphism** (`STUB`) — precise
  18-dimensional Lie-algebra equality
  $\mathrm{Aut}(\Tdiamond, \mathcal{A}=1) = SO(3,1) \times SU(3)
  \times SU(2) \times U(1)$ with explicit
  established-vs-extension decomposition.

### Three Tier-1/Tier-2 proof additions

- **§10.7.1 Born rule via Gleason** (Tier 1).  Addresses the
  necessity question for $|\psi|^2$ that v0.98-RC handled with a
  sufficiency argument only.  Gleason's theorem on the framework's
  emergent Hilbert space $\ell^2(\Tdiamond, \mathbb{C}^2)$ forces
  the quadratic form.
- **§7.7 explicit metric tensor** (Tier 2).  Closes the v0.98-RC
  "honest gap" with an explicit weak-field formula:
  $g_{\mu\nu} = \mathrm{diag}(-(\rho/\bar\rho)^{-2},
  (\rho/\bar\rho)^{2}\delta_{ij}) + O(\varphi^2/c^4)$.  Builds on
  the existing eq:boltzmann_clock derivation in §7.3.
- **§15 refined automorphism conjecture** (Tier 2).  Now stated as
  a precise 18-dimensional Lie-algebra equality with explicit
  decomposition: $SO(3,1)$ and $U(1)$ are *established* on the
  existing per-site $\mathbb{C}^2$ (computational verification
  shows per-site $SU(2)$ overlaps with the Lorentz rotation
  subgroup rather than forming a separate factor); $SU(2)_W$ and
  $SU(3)$ require per-site $\mathbb{C}^2$ isospin and $\mathbb{C}^3$
  colour structural additions.

### Six new computational scaffolding scripts (`src/utilities/`)

For the SM-derivation programme (follow-on #16):

- `automorphism_discrete.py` — discrete spatial $\mathrm{Aut}(\Gamma,
  V)$ is order 48 ($B_3 \cong O_h$ abstractly, 12 elements
  orthogonal in standard $\mathbb{R}^3$).
- `automorphism_rgb_su3.py` — existing RGB symmetry contributes
  only $\mathbb{Z}_3 \subset SU(3)$ (abelian → no continuous
  extension to $SU(3)$).
- `automorphism_direct_product.py` — per-site $SU(2)$ and Lorentz
  rotations are *the same generators* on existing $\mathbb{C}^2$.
- `automorphism_direct_product_extended.py` — on extended
  $\mathbb{C}^{12}$, all four conjecture factors commute pairwise;
  total dim 18 verified.
- `tick_rule_extended_consistency.py` — trivial tensor extension of
  the bipartite tick rule preserves $\mathcal{A}=1$, parity, and
  global $SU(2)_W \times SU(3)$ symmetries.
- `tick_rule_gauge_invariance.py` — gauge-coupled extension's
  matter bilinear and bipartite Wilson plaquette $V_1, -V_2, -V_1,
  V_2$ are gauge-invariant.
